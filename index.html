About Our Company

PHITECH Solutions is a technology-driven company specializing in high-voltage testing equipment and services. Based in Nashik, India, we deliver reliable, innovative, and safety-focused solutions for the power industry. Serving laboratories, manufacturers, and utilities, we provide end-to-end services—from design to deployment—ensuring precision, performance, and long-term value in every project we undertake.

Our Services

Annual Maintenance for Impulse Generators & GIS/AIS

Comprehensive annual upkeep ensuring optimal performance, reliability, and extended lifespan of impulse generators and GIS/AIS systems.

GIS/AIS System Repairs & Upkeep

Expert repair and preventive maintenance services to restore GIS/AIS systems to peak operational efficiency and minimize downtime.

Supply of GIS/AIS Consumables

Reliable source for premium GIS/AIS consumables, essential for seamless operation, assembly, and maintenance of high-voltage switchgear systems.

All Types of O-Rings for GIS/AIS

Precision-manufactured O-rings for all GIS/AIS models, ensuring proper sealing, pressure resistance, and gas-tight performance in critical applications.

GIS Assembly Consumables

Specialized consumables for GIS assembly processes, delivering compatibility, durability, and efficiency during installation and service tasks.

High-Strength Fasteners & Bolts

Industrial-grade fasteners and bolts designed for high-stress GIS applications, offering superior strength, corrosion resistance, and reliability.

What We Do

At Phitech Solutions, we specialize in delivering cutting-edge high-voltage testing systems, automation solutions, and electrical services for the power industry. We import advanced machinery from global manufacturers—primarily China—and assemble, configure, and optimize them in India to suit diverse industrial needs. From impulse generators and PD testing equipment to SF6 gas handling systems and Faraday cage setups, we provide complete, customized solutions for transformer, GIS, and cable testing. With precision engineering, reliable support, and a customer-first approach, we empower clients to improve efficiency, ensure safety, and meet international testing standards.

GIS/AIS System Repairs & Maintenance Services

At Phitech Solutions, we deliver end-to-end GIS/AIS repair and maintenance support, tailored for power utilities, industries, and OEMs across all voltage levels.

Experienced GIS Experts

Technicians with OEM-level experience ensure accurate diagnostics, efficient repairs, and reliable GIS system restoration.

Comprehensive Voltage Range Support

Service coverage from 33kV–765kV GIS/AIS with tailored preventive and corrective maintenance across voltage classes.

Fully Equipped with Tools & Tackles

Teams use calibrated tools: SF6 kits, vacuum pumps, detectors, and certified PPE for safe servicing.

Complete Erection & Manpower Support

Specialized manpower handles GIS/AIS erection, dismantling, testing, commissioning, shutdowns, and retrofits professionally and efficiently.

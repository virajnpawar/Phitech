About Us

SHORT HISTORY

Phitech Solutions began with a vision to revolutionize the high-voltage industry in India. Starting with global imports and local assembly, we’ve grown into a trusted partner for power infrastructure solutions. Our journey is defined by innovation, precision, and a commitment to delivering quality systems that meet the evolving demands of the energy sector—powering progress, one project at a time. Over the years, we’ve expanded our capabilities, introduced advanced testing systems, and built strong client relationships rooted in performance, integrity, and technical excellence. Today, we continue to push boundaries, setting new benchmarks in high-voltage engineering.

Our SOLUTIONS

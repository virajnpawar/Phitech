What We Do

At Phitech Solutions, we specialize in delivering cutting-edge high-voltage testing systems, automation solutions, and electrical services for the power industry. We import advanced machinery from global manufacturers—primarily China—and assemble, configure, and optimize them in India to suit diverse industrial needs. From impulse generators and PD testing equipment to SF6 gas handling systems and Faraday cage setups, we provide complete, customized solutions for transformer, GIS, and cable testing. With precision engineering, reliable support, and a customer-first approach, we empower clients to improve efficiency, ensure safety, and meet international testing standards.

GIS/AIS System Repairs & Maintenance Services

At Phitech Solutions, we deliver end-to-end GIS/AIS repair and maintenance support, tailored for power utilities, industries, and OEMs across all voltage levels.

Experienced GIS Experts

Technicians with OEM-level experience ensure accurate diagnostics, efficient repairs, and reliable GIS system restoration.

Comprehensive Voltage Range Support

Service coverage from 33kV–765kV GIS/AIS with tailored preventive and corrective maintenance across voltage classes.

Fully Equipped with Tools & Tackles

Teams use calibrated tools: SF6 kits, vacuum pumps, detectors, and certified PPE for safe servicing.

Complete Erection & Manpower Support

Specialized manpower handles GIS/AIS erection, dismantling, testing, commissioning, shutdowns, and retrofits professionally and efficiently.

Turnkey GIS Installation & Commissioning

From unloading to charging, we handle full GIS setup, testing, SF6 gas filling, and activation.

Supply of GIS/AIS Consumables

Phitech Solutions offers a comprehensive range of OEM-approved consumables vital for GIS/AIS erection, maintenance, and operational efficiency.

Molecular Sieves (Silica Gel)

High-efficiency moisture-absorbing material that keeps SF6 gas systems dry, ensuring insulation performance and system longevity.

High-Grade Lint-Free Tissue / Wipes

Non-abrasive wipes designed for precision cleaning of GIS parts without leaving lint, fibers, or static during service work.

Cleaning Agents (Isopropyl Alcohol - IPA)

Fast-evaporating cleaning solvent used to degrease and prepare metallic and insulating surfaces during installation and maintenance.

SF6 Gas (Ultra High Purity)

Certified SF6 gas for insulation and arc quenching in GIS systems, delivered with standard-compliant moisture and purity levels.

O-Rings & Sealing Gaskets

High-performance sealing elements available in EPDM, NBR, and Viton, designed for reliable SF6 gas retention and system integrity.

Contact Grease / Silicone Grease

Lubricant that improves electrical contact conductivity, prevents oxidation, and extends the life of joints and terminals.

Desiccant Bags & Breathers

Moisture control accessories used during storage and shipping to protect GIS components from humidity and condensation.

SF6 Gas Leak Detection Spray

Foaming agent used to visually detect even micro-level SF6 gas leaks during assembly and commissioning processes.

Shielding Tapes & Protective Wraps

Specialized EMI/RFI shielding tapes used on joints and cables to prevent electromagnetic interference and maintain signal integrity.

Copper & Aluminum Lugs / Termination Kits

Reliable termination accessories enabling strong, corrosion-resistant cable connections for safe GIS/AIS electrical interfacing.

Torque Seal / Marking Compounds

Applied to fasteners for visual torque verification, ensuring quality control and traceability during system audits and inspections.

PPE & Earthing Accessories

Essential safety gear including insulated gloves, grounding rods, and shorting cables to ensure safe GIS/AIS operation and servicing.
